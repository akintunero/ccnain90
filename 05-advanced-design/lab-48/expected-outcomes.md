## Expected Outcomes – Lab 46 Advanced Design and Automation

By completing this lab, learners should be able to:

1. **Produce a scalable multi-site addressing and VLAN plan**
   - Apply the master blueprint consistently across HQ and multiple branches.
   - Avoid overlapping or ambiguous IP assignments.
2. **Define reusable configuration patterns**
   - Identify common switch, router, and firewall roles.
   - Describe standard interface and VLAN configurations per role.
3. **Outline an automation-ready workflow**
   - Propose how inventory and addressing data are stored and version-controlled.
   - Describe how configuration templates are rendered and validated before deployment.
4. **Assess design trade-offs**
   - Discuss summarisation, redundancy, and failure domain considerations in the design.

These outcomes prepare learners for the remaining advanced design and automation-focused labs (47–50).
